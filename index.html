School temple (Saraswati temple)

The Saraswati Temple was established on 1st Feburary 2007.

The Mayo College Girlsâ€™ School temple was built on the 1st of February, 2007. Located opposite to the main building, the deity of all knowledge and learning- Maa Saraswati , is clearly visible to all who visit the school.

Girls of all religion visit and attend the temple service and it is very much a part of their daily routine.

School Library

Library at Mayo College Girls School (MCGS) has a vast collection of books that are easily accessible to the children. There is not one section left uncovered by the collection of books that we keep including English/Hindi Fiction, General Knowledge, Computer Science, Home Science, Art, Economics, Rajasthan culture, History, Geography, Physical education etc. Under the supervision of two exceptional librarians, the library is well maintained with all new releases available in stock. Each child holds a library card and all the books are Bar-coded. We keep a systematic record of the books being issued and returned by the children. Our library also provides the children with daily, weekly, and monthly issues of newspapers and magazines.
